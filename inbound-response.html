Response Shape

I can turn a first message into a concrete next step instead of a generic intro call. The most useful output is a short role/project fit read, risky assumptions, the smallest responsible first slice, the proof path that would show whether the work is real, and the right next route.

For roles

  • Role fit against the public proof route.
  • Likely first-month ownership.
  • Which repo, CI run, demo, or doc proves the closest match.

For projects

  • Smallest responsible working slice.
  • Integration, data, access, hosting, and handoff risks.
  • Verification path: tests, logs, screenshots, runbook, or demo.

For proof review

  • Relevant public proof links.
  • Claim-to-evidence map.
  • Gaps that need a deeper check before a decision.
