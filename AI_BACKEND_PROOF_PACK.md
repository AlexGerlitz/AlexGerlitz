# AI Backend Review Pack

Public route: https://alexgerlitz.github.io/AlexGerlitz/ai-backend-proof-pack.html

This is the compact review path for Python/backend automation, AI workflow, CRM/ERP/API integration, Docker/CI handoff, internal tools, and technical review. It routes technical review into Decision-Ready Contact before the PDF resume handoff.

## Core Signal

I do not just connect workflow nodes. I move state, RAG, approvals, audit, retries, idempotency, quality checks, and integration contracts into backend-owned systems, then leave CI, docs, runbooks, and reviewable evidence.

- Remote backend automation and integration role with Python, FastAPI, PostgreSQL, Docker, CI, integrations, or internal tools.

- Fixed-scope workflow project where one messy business process needs a working slice with tests, logs, docs, and handoff.

## Useful First Context

- One role, workflow, project, or technical problem.

- First success condition, timeline, constraints, and what must not break.
- Expected output: first slice, technical review, integration plan, or production hardening path.
