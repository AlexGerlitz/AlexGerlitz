First Reply Promise

Send one remote role, workflow, project, or technical review plus one success condition. I will answer with a fit read, risky assumptions, the smallest responsible first slice, review path to inspect, and the right next route: remote role, fixed-scope project, technical review, or no-fit.

Remote Role

Useful first context for a recruiter or hiring manager.

  • Role title, remote setup, team surface, and hiring timeline.
  • Product domain, stack, systems, and first-month ownership.
  • Current pain: speed, reliability, integrations, AI workflow, Docker/CI handoff, internal tools, or support load.
  • Vacancy, product, docs, or technical description if public.

Fixed-Scope Project

Useful first context for a founder, operator, or technical owner.

  • Business process, current tools, data sources, APIs, databases, documents, or chats.
  • Practical definition of done and what must not break.
  • Closest evidence-backed offer route or employer trigger.
  • Review path to reference first: AI Ops Business Scenario Replay or DriveDesk AI Operator route.
  • Required output: working slice, integration adapter, AI workflow, internal tool, Docker/CI fix, runbook, or recovery plan.
  • Deadline, commercial constraints, access limits, hosting, compliance, language, timezone, and handoff depth.

Technical Review

Useful first context when the decision depends on evidence.

  • Repository, demo, system link, architecture page, CI run, or public evidence target.
  • Claim, risk, failure mode, or unclear boundary to validate.
  • Safe logs, screenshots, error text, recent changes, and current success check.
  • What must keep working while the review is happening.
